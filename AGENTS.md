- **Production guardrails:** Playground — **not production**. Don't depend on it from a product repo.

Follow the standards' **git workflow** (branch → conventional commit → verify → stop),
the **design-system rules** (tokens only, two skins, green = signal), and the
**honesty rule** (real figures or a labeled target — never invent proof). Don't
cross repo boundaries — use the published interface.
